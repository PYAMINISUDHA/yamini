Srikurmam Temple

Srikurmam temple is known for its distinctive architectural style. The design of the gopuram is different from the regular style seen at other Vaishnavite temples. It also has two dhvajasthambas, one on the west and the other on the east, which is another rare element in a Vaishnavite temple.

Kalingapatnam Beach

Kalingapatnam Beach is a scenic shoreline located in the Srikakulam district of Andhra Pradesh, India. It is known for its natural beauty, clear waters, and tranquil environment, which makes it a desirable destination for tourists looking to relax and enjoy coastal surroundings..

Salihundam

Salihundam is one of the main Buddhist excavation sites, showing evidence that Buddhism thrived in the local area during the 2nd and 3rd centuries..

Srimukhalingam Temple

Srimukhalingeswara Temple is a temple dedicated to Lord Shiva located on the left banks of River Vamsadhara. Elegantly carved, this temple is a group of three temples dedicated to three forms of Lord Shiva namely, Mukhalingeswara, Bhimeswara and Someswara.
